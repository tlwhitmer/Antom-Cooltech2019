Aqualizer

What is Aqualizer?

The Aqualizer is a product created by Antom for people who are getting sickness due to swimming in dirty swimming pools. This product will notify its owner when their pool is dirty through an app or the color of the light which will change based on how clean their pool is.

Is it Worth buying the Aqualizer?

It is definitely worth buying our product because it would help decrease recreational illnesses in dirty swimming pools. Most people can't see certain dirt particles inside of a pool; Which is why our product would be used for good so children and adults won't to get sick!
