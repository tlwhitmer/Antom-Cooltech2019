About Aqualizer:

The Aqualizer is a product created by the Antom team for the people's sake. It helps benefit owners that has a swimming pool to get notified when to clean their pool;either by a text or the changes of the pool light! The colors blue, red, green, and yellow are the 4 original colors that will be in the pool light, but you can also custom make what colors you want it to have as well.

where can you get Aqualize?

You can get the Aqualizer in stores that sell Pool accessories.

When will the Aqualizer be released?

Aqualizer will be official released in stores on May 30th, 2019!
